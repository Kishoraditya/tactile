Tactile Audio Performance 


STEP 1: APP OPENING (HOME SCREEN)
For blind users: 
Avatar will start speaking just audio “Hello my friend! I am your tooth-care buddy.
I will help you keep your teeth clean and strong.
To begin, tap once anywhere on the screen.”

STEP 2: AGE GROUP SELECTION 
Three options displayed as ( along with audio reading all options)
1)	Group A : 1 to 4 years 
2)	Group B : 5 to 11 years 
3)	Groups C: 12 to 18 years
User will speak the option from above to select the group also care giver can select manually or verbally.
Once selected:
•	Short vibration confirmation
•	Avatar says:
“Good choice! Let us start brushing together.”

DETAILED AVATAR SCRIPT – AGE WISE
AGE GROUP 1: 1–4 YEARS (Parent-assisted brushing)
Avatar Personality
•	Very soft
•	Singing tone
•	Slow pace
Opening:
“Hello little star!
I am your tooth fairy friend.
Are you ready to clean your shiny teeth?”
Instruction to Parent or caregiver: “Dear parent, please help your child hold the toothbrush.”

Brushing Flow (Total time: 1 minute)
Cue 1 – Toothpaste
“Put a tiny rice-grain toothpaste on the brush.
Tap when ready.”
Cue 2 – Start brushing
Soft music begins 
“Let us brush the front teeth.
Move the brush up and down”
(10 seconds)
Soft bell sound and vibration 
Vibration cue
“Now let us brush the side teeth.
Round and round… like a circle.”
(20 seconds)
End Message
“Yay! Your teeth are happy.
Give yourself a clap!
Come back in the morning and night.”




AGE GROUP 2: 5–11 YEARS
Avatar Personality
Friendly teacher
Encouraging
Uses imagination (superhero, shield)

Brushing Flow (Total time: 2 minutes)

START
“Hello champion!
Today, we will protect your teeth from germs.
Hold your toothbrush like a pencil.”

STEP-WISE COMMANDS 
Step 1: Toothpaste
“Put a pea-sized toothpaste on your brush.
Double tap when ready.”
Step 2: Upper Teeth – Outer Side (30 sec)
Vibration starts
“Place the brush on the upper teeth.
Move in small circles.”
Soft rhythmic beat continues
Step 3: Lower Teeth – Outer Side (30 sec)
“Good job!
Now brush the lower teeth the same way.”
Step 4: Inside Surfaces (30 sec)“Now brush inside teeth.
Small circles… slow and gentle.”
Step 5: Chewing Surfaces (20 sec)“Brush the chewing surfaces.
Back and forth.”
Step 6: Tongue (10 sec)“Brush your tongue gently.
Once or twice only.”
END MOTIVATION
“Fantastic!
Your smile is stronger today.
Remember: brush twice every day.”

AGE GROUP 3: 12–18 YEARS
Avatar Personality
Calm
Respectful
Coach-like

START
“Hello!
Oral hygiene keeps your teeth, gums, and breath healthy.
Let us begin a complete brushing routine.”
BRUSHING FLOW (2–3 minutes)
Step 1: Toothpaste
“Apply a pea-sized fluoride toothpaste.”
Step 2: Modified Bass Technique (Simplified)
“Place the brush at the gum line.
Tilt slightly.
Small vibrating strokes.”
(30 sec upper, 30 sec lower)
Step 3: Inner Surfaces
“Brush inside surfaces carefully.”
30 sec
Step 4: Occlusal Surfaces
“Brush chewing surfaces thoroughly.
30 sec
Step 5: Tongue
“Clean the tongue gently to prevent bad breath.”
10 sec
END MESSAGE
“Well done.
Consistent brushing prevents cavities and gum disease.
See you tonight.”
